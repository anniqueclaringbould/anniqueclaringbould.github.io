**Annique Claringbould** · Principal Investigator

Annique Claringbould is interested in researching the changes in gene regulation and gene expression that underlie complex diseases. She joined the Department of Internal Medicine at Erasmus Medical Center in July 2024. During her PhD at the University Medical Centre in Groningen, Annique studied how genetic variants affect gene expression using large population cohort data. She then pursued postdoctoral research at the European Molecular Biology Laboratories in Heidelberg, Germany, focusing on single-cell gene regulatory networks and multiomics approaches.
