**Annique Claringbould** · Principal Investigator

Research focuses on understanding gene regulation and gene expression in complex diseases, combining population genomics, single-cell multiomics, and gene regulatory network approaches — with a particular focus on type 2 diabetes and familial hypercholesterolaemia.
