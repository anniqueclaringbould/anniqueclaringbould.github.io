**May Krause** · PhD Student

May Krause joined our research group in May 2025, after completing her master's at the University of Cape Town. She is investigating the gene regulatory mechanisms that underlie type 2 diabetes, focusing on linking polygenic risk to β-cell regulatory circuitry at single-cell resolution, using iPSC-derived pancreatic cells combined with chromatin accessibility and gene expression profiling to identify transcription factors and gene regulatory networks involved in T2D risk.
