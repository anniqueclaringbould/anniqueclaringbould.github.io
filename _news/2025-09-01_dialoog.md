Last summer, I was interviewed by the [Dialoog magazine](https://formulieren.diabetesfonds.nl/dialoog), the donor magazine of the Dutch Diabetes Foundation. 
The [article](assets/pdf/Dialoog2_2025_DEF.pdf) (in Dutch) touches on how heritable differences influence the chance to develop diabetes, and the potential consequences for personalised medicine.
